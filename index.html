ABOUT


Why would you choose any other school when India’s most unique school, the FIRST & ONLY recognized online school in India, is coming home to your child? A school that brings together a ‘real’ interactive curriculum delivered on an easy-to-use platform, a wide range of extracurricular activities including counseling, yoga, music, art, dance, story-telling, coding, cultural exchanges, personality-development classes & moral studies, post-classroom learning content, in a safe and engaging learning environment with 3-4 hours of regular online classes 5-6 days a week in small class sizes. We try to keep our class size small (mostly up to 15 students/class), this way your child gets dedicated attention, your child will be getting so much more than any other school could provide. Plus, your child will be studying in India’s most multicultural classrooms comprising students from all parts of India. It is time you focus on your own work and leave your child’s schooling in trusted hands because in K8 School you will not have to sit in your child’s classes as the learning management system is powerful enough to inspire children to learn easily and independently. No matter what your child’s career goal is, K8 School would give enough time to your child to do well in studies and simultaneously devote time to other goals, hobbies and interests. We are not a traditional school offering online classes, we are a 100% online school, FIRST & ONLY accredited online school in India. We are building the future of school education in India and your child deserves to be an active participant in this journey!
